We Serve the Ocean

We hope to organize programs for beach cleaning as well as conservation of marine diversity. Members who wish to join us will also have the opportunity to join these programs, and we hope to raise funds, labor and supplies for members who organise such activities.
Ocean must be protected as it serves the earth as a major oxygen source. To protect the ocean it's beauty and its diverse nature must be kept intact, for our younger generation and for the ocean life.
